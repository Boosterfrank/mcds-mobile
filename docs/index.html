MCDS Mobile – Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

MCDS Mobile is an independent open-source app providing secure mobile access to the MySchoolApp student portal. This app does not track users, sell data, or connect to third-party services. All requests are made directly to the official MySchoolApp servers via HTTPS, and no information leaves your device.

Information We Collect

  • Session Cookies: Used temporarily to fetch your assignments, grades, and schedule from MySchoolApp. Not stored permanently.
  • Local Data: Stored only on your device; cleared when you sign out or delete the app.
  • Preview Mode: Uses fake demo data for testing. No login required.

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to display school information within the app. No analytics, tracking, or external data sharing occurs.

Security

All traffic is encrypted with TLS 1.3. No passwords or personal information are transmitted outside of the MySchoolApp system.

Children’s Privacy

The app follows COPPA guidelines and does not collect personal data beyond what is provided by the school’s secure portal.
