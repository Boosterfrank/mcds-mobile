MCDS Mobile – Support

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to the official support page for MCDS Mobile, the unofficial companion app for the Miami Country Day School Portal.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

  • Login Issues: Ensure you have an active MySchoolApp session before using the app. If your login expires, sign out and sign in again.
  • Data Not Loading: Check your internet connection and confirm the MySchoolApp site is accessible.
  • App Updates: Visit the App Store and ensure you have the latest version installed.

📩 Contact Developer

If you encounter bugs, crashes, or missing information, please reach out:

  • Email: francobetancourt@gmail.com
  • GitHub: Submit an Issue

💡 Feature Requests

Have an idea to improve the app? You can request new features by opening an issue on GitHub Issues with the tag feature-request.

🔒 Privacy & Data

Your data privacy matters. Read our full Privacy Policy to learn how information is handled.
